Brief Biography:

He is now holding the Nanyang Assistant Professorship at NTU, and also a Cluster Director (Smart Grids and Microgrids) at Energy Research Institute @ NTU (ERI@N). Prior to joining NTU, he held the University of Sydney Postdoctoral Fellowship at University of Sydney, Australia . His research areas include power system, smart grid, and renewable energy integration. He has published 110 SCI-indexed journal papers (including 70 in IEEE Transactions and 24 in IET journals), as well as over 60 international conference papers. He has 6 “web-of-science highly cited papers” and 9 IEEE/IET paper contest awards and conference best paper awards. His research deliverables have been practically applied by power utilities in Australia, USA, Hong Kong, Macau, and China Mainland.
In Singapore, his research is funded by National Research Foundation (NRF), Ministry of Education (MOE), Energy Market Authority (EMA), Housing & Development Board (HDB), DSO National Laboratories, Rolls-Royce, etc. He has also been collaborating with a number of universities in Australia, China, Hong Kong, UK, Denmark, France, Switzerland, US.
Dr Xu is an Editor for IEEE Transactions on Smart Grid, a committee member for IEEE PES Singapore Chapter, CSEE Journal of Power and Energy Systems, and IET Generation, Transmission & Distribution.

Particular Research Interests:
1) Renewable-energy power system's stability, control, and optimization
2) Data-analytics for smart grid applications
3) Microgrid and multi-energy system
4) Electric vehicle to grid (V2G)
